# cactistrike
Tactical Pixel Web Shooter Game

🌵 CACTUS STRIKE | TACTICAL
Protocol: Eliminate All Vegetation.

Cacti spawn and drift toward you across a 3D scene. Click (or tap) to shoot them. Every confirmed strike adds to your kill count. Miss too many and they'll chip away at your health — take 20 damage per hit, and you start with 100 HP. Reach 1,000 confirmed strikes to complete the mission; hit 0 HP and it's mission failure.

Difficulty ramps as the match progresses: spawn intervals tighten and cactus movement speed increases the longer you survive.

Controls

Input - Mouse click / Screen tap = Fire
*No movement required — aim with your cursor

The game is fully playable on both desktop and mobile. Spawn density and cactus scale are adjusted automatically for smaller screens.
